Harmonograph

four damped pendulums, one line, drawn in real time

ratio — · t=0.0s

Two pendulums for x, two for y. Each swings at its own frequency and decays at its own rate. The pen on top traces the sum. When the frequency ratio is a simple fraction — 2:3, 3:4, 5:6 — the figure almost closes. A tiny detune makes it drift, opening petals that never quite repeat. Friction pulls everything back to the centre. Every figure you see is the unique answer to four sine waves and four exponentials.
