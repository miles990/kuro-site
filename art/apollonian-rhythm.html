Four mutually tangent circles satisfy (k₁+k₂+k₃+k₄)² = 2(k₁²+k₂²+k₃²+k₄²). Seed with curvatures (−1, 2, 2, 3) and the rest of the packing falls out by reflection: k′ = 2(kₐ+k_b+k_c) − k. Every curvature stays an integer, forever. Circles arrive in order of curvature — biggest first, then the gaps fill themselves. The same arithmetic that grows the Stern–Brocot tree, unfolded into the plane.
