The Stern–Brocot Garden

every positive rational, exactly once — a mediant tree grown live

depth 0 · 0 fractions

Start with two seeds you cannot reach: 0/1 and 1/0. Between any two neighbours a/b and c/d, drop their mediant (a+c)/(b+d) — always already in lowest terms. Repeat. Every positive rational appears once, and never twice. The tree is the proof.
